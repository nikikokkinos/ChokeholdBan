What is the Chokehold Ban?

  • NYC Council Bill to classify chokeholds used by NYPD as a misdemeanor offense
  • Bill was first introduced in 2014 after Eric Garner was choked and killed by NYPD
  • Chokeholds are currently banned by internal NYPD guidelines
  • An independent investigation by NYC police inspector general in 2015 found that
    chokeholds are used and usually officers don't face reprecussions
